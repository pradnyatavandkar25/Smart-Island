About Smart Island


Smart Island – Smarter Edge Control for Your Android Smart Island transforms your Android experience with a futuristic Dynamic Island–style interface inspired by modern flagship devices.

Key Features


1. Dynamic Island Notifications
View incoming messages, charging status, and alerts in a floating pill design.Real-time animations for calls, music, and timers.

2. Smart Edge Assistant
Floating interactive edge for quick controls like brightness, volume, or recent apps.Gesture-based actions for smoother navigation.

3. Music Visualization & Controls
Floating music bar with live wave animation.Supports media control for popular players (Spotify, YouTube, etc.).

4. Notification Pop-ups
Clean pop-up bubbles for messages and system updates without interrupting your work.

5. Battery & Charging Indicator
Animated charging ring with progress and percentage display.

6. Call Display & Quick Reply
Mini call UI with accept/reject options in a dynamic bubble.

7. Customization Options
Adjust island size, position, theme, and animation style.Dark and light themes for battery optimization.

8. Performance Optimized
Lightweight, battery-efficient, and designed for smooth performance.Uses Android’s foreground service to ensure reliability.

Permissions Used


  • Microphone – For features like audio visualization.
  • Modify Audio Settings – To manage volume and sound effects.
  • Foreground Service – Keeps Smart Island running seamlessly in the background.
  • Query All Packages – To integrate notifications from multiple apps.
